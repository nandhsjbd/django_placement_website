About Geethanjali Institute of Science and Technology

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure

Geethanjali Institute of Science and Technology boasts a modern, well-equipped campus that spans over 50 acres. The institution is built to provide students with an environment conducive to learning and innovation. Classrooms are equipped with smart boards, comfortable seating, and ample natural light, fostering an ideal academic atmosphere.

High-Speed Wi-Fi Facilities

The entire campus is Wi-Fi enabled, ensuring that students have access to the internet for academic and personal needs. With speeds up to 100 Mbps, the Wi-Fi service allows students to stay connected, complete research, and access learning materials from anywhere on campus.

Comprehensive Library

The library at Geethanjali Institute offers a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. With over 50,000 books and access to online databases, students can engage in in-depth research and learning. The library also has a quiet reading room for those who prefer peaceful study environments.

Beautiful Garden and Green Spaces

The campus is surrounded by lush green gardens and landscaped areas that provide a serene environment for relaxation and study. The well-maintained botanical garden features a variety of plants and trees, contributing to the overall aesthetic appeal of the institution.

Playground and Sports Facilities

Geethanjali Institute believes in the holistic development of students. The campus features a large playground for various sports such as cricket, football, and athletics. In addition, there are indoor sports facilities for badminton, table tennis, and chess, allowing students to pursue their athletic passions.

Efficient Bus Facility

The institute provides a comprehensive transportation service for students and faculty members. With a fleet of over 20 buses, the service covers major parts of the city and surrounding areas, ensuring that everyone can commute comfortably and safely.

Dedicated Placement Cell

The Placement Cell at Geethanjali Institute is highly active, with a dedicated team that works tirelessly to connect students with top recruiters. With a focus on employability, the cell provides training sessions, workshops, and career guidance, ensuring that students are well-prepared to enter the workforce. The placement cell has successfully placed students in reputed companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and Cognizant.
